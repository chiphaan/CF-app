Exercises

Some airway techniques need no equipment, and focus on breathing exercises to clear mucus:

1- Active Cycle of Breathing Techniques (ACBT) – a cycle of deep breathing, huffing, coughing and relaxed breathing to move mucus.

2- Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP) – breathing out through a mask or mouthpiece against a resistance to build up pressure behind mucus to move it.

3- Autogenic Drainage (AD) – moves mucus from smaller to bigger airways using controlled breathing at different levels. Uses fast flow rates as you sigh out but ensures the airways are kept open.
